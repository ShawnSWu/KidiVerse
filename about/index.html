/kiːdiːvɜːrs/ KidiVerse / A Universe of Memories

In Taiwanese Hokkien, "Kidi" means memory. Combine that with "Verse" from universe, and you get KidiVerse: a galaxy of thoughts, notes, and knowledge fragments orbiting in the space of my mind.

This site is a map of that inner universe—built by weaving together my Obsidian Markdown notes and Canvas visualizations. Every entry is a star, every thought a little planet. Some are fully formed, others still nebulas in progress.

It's not just a website—it's the evolving shape of how I think, learn, and remember.
